Three theorem proving conferences, FroCoS, ITP and TABLEAUX will happen colocated in Reykjavik, Iceland, in 2025.

The conferences will be hosted by ICE-TCS, the theoretical computer science lab of Reykjavik University.

Local organization chair: Tarmo Uustalu. Team: Antonis Achilleos, Bjarki Gunnarsson, Vasiliki Kyriakou, Calvin Santiago Lee, Yasuaki Morita, Jacob Neumann.

The Iceproof project and the Artificial Intelligence journal support the conference.
