Registration

Registration is open. We have aimed to make the fees low, but Iceland is an expensive country.

You can choose from among the following items for in-person participation: TABLEAUX (3 days Sat-Mon), FroCoS (3 days Mon-Wed), TABLEAUX+FroCoS (5 days Sat-Wed), ITP (4 days Sun-Wed), Rocq (1 day Sat), Lean (1 day Thu).

All of these fees include coffee breaks and lunches during the days paid for. Full rates also include the conference dinner (Mon evening). There are early vs late rates, regular vs reduced (student/retired) rates and the saver option of not including the conference dinner.

Students and retired participants are entitled to reduced rates. Please provide a proof by email.

Early registration is until 25 August. From 26 August, late registration rates apply.

One author of every accepted paper of every constituent event must pay a regular (= non-reduced) in-person attendance fee. It need not be the presenter: for example a student may present the paper and pay a reduced fee if a supervisor will also attend and pay a regular fee. This applies to both the three conferences as well as the workshops.

Cancellation will be possible up until 2 weeks before the start of the conference (until 13 September), but we will withhold a 100 EUR administrative fee.

Online participation is free, but still requires registration. We are grateful for a small donation (appearing as any other fee on the ticket and receipt).

Every paper accepted by FroCoS, ITP or TABLEAUX must be presented in person, unless there are special circumstances in the picture and online presentation has been agreed with the organizers by the early registration deadline of 25 August.

Registration is with Fienta. Registration page at Fienta..

You get can get a receipt from Fienta's system, but you need to pay attention, else you won't get one (will only get tickets). You can specify the payer data you need (eg your organization) in the "Payment method" section toward the end of the interaction session. For this, check the box "I would like a receipt after payment" and fill out the relevant details (organization, address, reg code, VAT number, other information you want on the invoice).
