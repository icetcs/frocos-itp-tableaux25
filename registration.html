Registration

Registration will open soon. We are striving to make the fees low, but Iceland is an expensive country.

Here is what you can expect.

You will be able to choose between the following for in-person participation: TABLEAUX (3 days Sat-Mon), FroCoS (3 days Mon-Wed), TABLEAUX+FroCoS (5 days Sat-Wed), ITP (4 days Sun-Wed), Rocq+ITP (5 days Sat-Wed), ITP+Lean (5 days Sun-Thu), Rocq only (1 day Sat), Lean only (1 day Thu).

By default all of these fees include coffee breaks and lunches during the days paid for. There will be early vs late rates, full vs reduced (student/retired) rates and the option of including the conference dinner Mon evening.

One author of every accepted paper must pay the full fee. It need not be the presenter: for example a student may present the paper and pay the reduced fee if a supervisor will also attend and pay the full fee.

Cancellation will be possible up until 2 weeks before the start of the conference conference (until 13 September), but we will withhold a 100 EUR administrative fee.

Online participation will most likely be completely free, but still require registration.

Notice, however, that every paper accepted by FroCoS, ITP or TABLEAUX must be presented in person, unless there are special circumstances in the picture and online presentation has been agreed with the organizers before the early registration deadline.
